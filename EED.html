Electrical Engineering Department

About

The department of electrical engineering is one of the eight departments in the Institute of Engineering and Technology and offers degrees at the Bachelor, Master and PhD level. It was established in 1984 and is recognised by AICTE, New Delhi. The department is one of the largest and oldest departments in the Institute and has a well-qualified faculty, all holding degrees from the renowned institute in India. The Department of Electrical Engineering is committed to growing further as a socially responsible and research-oriented centre of learning.

Vision

To outshine in edification, research and industrial services in electrical engineering in tune with societal aspirations.

Mission

1. Impart value education to produce globally competent electrical engineers proficient of extending industrial services

2. Engage in research & progress in cutting edge and sustainable technologies.

3. Nurture scientific personality, professional ethics and industrial cooperation.

About EES

Electrical Engineering Society is the oldest student society of the Institute of Engineering and Technology Lucknow, working under the Electrical Engineering Department of the institute for enhancing the technical skills of the students.


Since its inception, EES has benchmarked its efforts in providing a platform to the students of the department for exposure into the field of electrical engineering beyond the syllabus and curriculum, by arranging workshops, expert lectures and industrial visits. Maintaining its legacy of knowledge transfer from one year to another, EES bridges the gap of learning between the senior and junior students and provides a positive environment for mutual development with the help of various interaction sessions and by operating a departmental library.


The core objectives and works of EES are in the direction to comply with the vision and mission of the Electrical Engineering Department and to bring out the best in the students of the department by arranging for necessary aids required for their development.


If we bounce back to the history of IET Lucknow at that time when no other societies or clubs were functioning, EES organized all technical and cultural events to evolve the students to the finest. And also 'Parakram' the annual technical fest of IET was organized by this society.


As due to resistors there is a voltage drop in the circuit, similarly due to some reasons flotation of participating current stopped and this society became inactive in some near past year. The day dawneth came, so was the scenario with EES and came back into action. By the efforts of students of EE department, a batch of 2016-20 the society was finally re-established on 22 Feb 2018 in the presence of Prof. Kuldeep Sahay, the honourable HOD of the Electrical Engineering Department, senior faculty members respected Prof. Bharti Dwivedi and Dr Satyendra Singh, Dr Pushkar Tripathi, Dr Arunima Verma and Faculty Adviser of EES, Dr Nitin Anand Srivastava.
